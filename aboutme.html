About Me

My name is Isaac Guerrero, and I am a senior IT major at NJIT.I attended Passaic High School and then pursued my education at Bergen Community College for two years. After completing my two years there, I transferred to NJIT. During my time at NJIT, I've had the opportunity to meet many individuals who share similar interests and hobbies. I've taken several classes that I find particularly interesting, including IS331, IT202, and IT490. Additionally, I am proud to be a part of the e-board of ACM, the largest computing club at NJIT. Through my involvement, I've forged valuable connections, made numerous friends, and had the chance to organize events such as the school's annual Hackathon and JCTF.
